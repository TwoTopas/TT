## 信号1: OpusClip 付费用户半年做不出10条好视频
**来源：** Product Hunt Aleksandar Šeter 真实评论
**原文：** "Use this service for half year... I didn't made 10 good reels for half year... I made mistake, but buy it for a year"
**核心痛点：**
- 非英语内容支持烂（克罗地亚语混Cyrillic/Latin脚本）
- 字幕编辑——不能控制断行位置，双字国家名随机断行

- 产出reels"看起来不像最火的那些"
- 结论：Captions 比它好
**付费状态：** 已付年费（后悔了）
**含义：** 非英语创作者的短视频工具需求真实存在。有人付了$XXX的年费但工具没满足。这是真实支付行为的信号。

## 信号2: 小企业软件堆栈过载
**来源：** Reddit r/smallbusiness 帖子 "What's your business software stack in 2026?"
**原文：** "A simple stack has worked better over time. One CRM, one email tool, and something for basic reporting. Every time more tools were added, usage dropped."
**另一条（r/shopifyDev）：** "It's the small daily stuff: messy integrations, tracking mismatches, clunky returns, and too many apps doing half-jobs."
**含义：** 小企业被过度工具化折磨。不是"需要更多工具"的市场，是"需要更少但更好的工具"的市场。

## 信号3: AI 实施差距
**来源：** Reddit r/aiToolForBusiness "What are small businesses realistically doing with AI in 2026?"
**原文：** "I feel like there's a huge gap between what AI promises and what small businesses can implement in their workflow."
**含义：** 不是AI不够强，是小企业没人能把它落地到日常流程里。
