## Core Structure — Community Operations SOP (Chinese Original → Adapted)

### 1. Community Tiering System (Chinese → Western)

| Chinese | Western Equivalent |
|---------|-------------------|
| 鱼塘群 (Fishpond — free, low engagement) | Free tier — email list, public Discord, open community |
| 中转群 (Transit — warm leads) | Trial tier — limited-access channels, 7-day trial |
| 核心群 (Core — paid members) | Paid tier — full access, VIP channels, coaching calls |

### 2. Daily Ops → Weekly Rhythm (Key Adaptation)

**Original Chinese Model** (hourly WeChat schedule):
- 7:30-8:30 — Morning greeting + news share
- 11:00 — Product education + social proof
- 13:00-16:00 — Interactive activity (check-in, quiz)
- 17:00-18:00 — Behind-the-scenes content
- 19:30 — Evening value post + product recommendation

**Western Adaptation** (weekly Discord/Circle rhythm):
- Monday — Educational content (tutorial, guide, resource)
- Tuesday — Community spotlight (member wins, showcases)
- Wednesday — Interactive (AMA, Q&A, poll, challenge)
- Thursday — Behind-the-scenes / culture post
- Friday — Weekly roundup + weekend preview
- Weekend — Light engagement (off-topic, games)

### 3. Member Journey (Chinese → Western)

| Phase | Chinese Approach | Western Adaptation |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Welcome | Group rules + red packet + 1-on-1 follow | Welcome message → role selection → introduction thread → first-week onboarding sequence |
| Activation | Daily check-ins, group tasks | "Aha moment" design: complete profile, first post, first reaction |
| Retention | Value posts + exclusive offers | Weekly content rhythm + community events + personal outreach |
| Growth | Referral incentives + group fission | Member-get-member program + social sharing + ambassador program |
| Monetization | Flash sales + group buying + course upsell | Subscription tiers + digital products + coaching |

### 4. Chinese Engagement Tactics NOT to Use

| Tactic | Why Not | Replace With |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Red packet (红包) | Not a Western practice | Welcome discount, free first month |
| Seeded members (群托) | Perceived as deceptive | Ambassador/champion program, transparently recruited |
| Group buying (拼团) | Different purchase psychology | Referral discount, bring-a-friend |
| QR code chain | WeChat-specific | Referral link, landing page |
| Moments mining | WeChat-specific | Social listening, content repurposing |
| Group announcement bots | WeChat-specific | Discord announcement channel, Circle pinned posts, email |

### 5. Welcome Sequence Pattern (Adapted)

**Chinese 7-wave welcome** (all in one group session):
1. Red packet #1 (ice breaker)
2. 1-cent flash sale
3. VIP content share
4. Mini game (red packet #2)
5. New product launch
6. Group purchase offer
7. Close with special deal

**Western 7-day onboarding** (spread over first week):
Day 1: Welcome + introduce yourself thread
Day 2: Start here guide + resources
Day 3: Community guidelines + roles
Day 4: First engagement prompt (poll, question)
Day 5: Member spotlight or showcase
Day 6: Upcoming events + calendar
Day 7: Feedback + what's next

### 6. Metrics Tracking (Chinese → Western)

| Chinese Metric | Western Equivalent |
|----------------|-------------------|
| 新增人数 (new members added) | Member acquisition rate |
| 活跃度 (activity rate) | DAU/MAU ratio, engagement rate |
| 转化率 (conversion rate) | Free→paid conversion rate |
| 复购率 (repurchase rate) | Renewal rate, retention rate |
| 客单价 (average order) | ARPU (average revenue per user) |
| GMV (gross merchandise value) | MRR/ARR (monthly/annual recurring revenue) |
| 退群率 (group leave rate) | Churn rate |
| K值 (viral coefficient) | K-factor, viral coefficient |
