## How to Use This Product

### Step 1: Set up your Discord server
Open [Community Launch Checklist](templates/01-community-launch-checklist.csv) and work through it before you invite anyone. It covers server setup (channels, roles, bots), content prep, and launch logistics. Start with 5-6 channels — you can always add more as your community grows.

### Step 2: Design your onboarding
Use the [Member Onboarding Flow](templates/02-member-onboarding-flow.csv) to build your welcome sequence. Set up a welcome message with Carl-bot or MEE6, create an #introductions channel, and plan your first 14 days of member engagement.

### Step 3: Plan your content rhythm
The [Weekly Content Calendar](templates/03-weekly-content-calendar.csv) keeps you consistent. Block out daily themes (Educational Monday, Spotlight Tuesday, etc.) and stay ahead of the "what do I post?" scramble.

### Step 4: Track engagement and segment members
Use the [Engagement Score Tracker](templates/04-engagement-score-tracker.csv) and [Member Segmentation Matrix](templates/05-member-segmentation-matrix.csv) to spot at-risk members early and identify power users for ambassador programs. On Discord, use MEE6 or Carl-bot leveling data to feed into your scoring.

### Step 5: Measure and optimize
The [Community KPI Dashboard](templates/08-community-kpi-dashboard.csv) is your weekly command center. Pair it with the [Feedback Collection System](templates/09-feedback-collection-system.csv) to close the loop between what members say and what you do.

**Pro tip:** Don't fill everything out on day one. Start with the Launch Checklist and Onboarding Flow. Add the Content Calendar when you start posting. Bring in the rest as you grow. Update all active templates every Friday — 30 minutes of admin saves hours of scrambling.

### Complete Tier: Read the chapters that match your stage
- Just starting? → Chapter 1 (Strategy)
- Engagement flat? → Chapter 2 (Engagement Layer System)
- Losing members? → Chapter 4 (Retention)
- Growing fast? → Chapter 5 (Referral & Growth)
- Making money? → Chapter 6 (Monetization)
