# Community Health Score Quiz

**A 5-question self-assessment to score your Discord server from 0-100.**

Takes 3 minutes. Answer each question, tally your points, and share your score.

---

## Question 1: Server Size
How many active members does your community have?

- **A)** 0-50 members → **0 points**
- **B)** 51-200 members → **5 points**
- **C)** 201-1,000 members → **10 points**
- **D)** 1,000+ members → **15 points**

## Question 2: Engagement Rate
What percentage of your members participate (post, react, or reply) at least once per week?

- **A)** Less than 10% → **0 points**
- **B)** 10-25% → **5 points**
- **C)** 26-40% → **10 points**
- **D)** More than 40% → **15 points**

## Question 3: Retention Rate
What percentage of members who joined 30 days ago are still active today?

- **A)** Less than 20% → **0 points**
- **B)** 20-49% → **5 points**
- **C)** 50-69% → **10 points**
- **D)** 70%+ → **15 points**

## Question 4: Monetization
Are you generating revenue from your community?

- **A)** No revenue yet → **0 points**
- **B)** Sporadic income (tips, donations) → **5 points**
- **C)** Consistent monthly revenue ($100-$999/mo) → **10 points**
- **D)** Significant revenue ($1,000+/mo) → **15 points**

## Question 5: Content Cadence
How consistent is your content publishing schedule?

- **A)** No schedule — I post whenever I remember → **0 points**
- **B)** 2-3 times per week, loosely planned → **5 points**
- **C)** Daily on weekdays with a consistent theme → **10 points**
- **D)** Multiple times daily with a documented content calendar → **15 points**

---

## Scoring

| Total Points | Health Score | Rating |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| 60-75 | 80-100 | 🟢 Thriving |
| 40-55 | 53-73 | 🟡 Solid |
| 20-35 | 27-47 | 🟠 Developing |
| 0-15 | 0-20 | 🔴 Fragile |

**To calculate your Health Score:** Total Points ÷ 75 × 100 = Score out of 100.

Example: 45 points ÷ 75 × 100 = **60/100** (Solid)
