# Community Health Score
## Generate a shareable 0-100 score for your Discord community

Take this 10-question assessment to generate your Community Health Score. Each question scores 0-10. Total = your score out of 100. **Share your result on social media** and compare with other server owners.

---

### How to Use
1. Answer each question honestly based on your community's current state
2. Add up your score (each question: 0-10, 0 = needs major work, 10 = exceptional)
3. Use the shareable template to post your result

---

### The 10-Point Assessment

| # | Question | 0-10 |
|---|----------|------|
| 1 | **Active Engagement** — What % of your members participate (post, react, reply) at least once per week? (0 = <10%, 5 = ~30%, 10 = 50%+) | |
| 2 | **Onboarding Quality** — Do new members get a clear welcome sequence and reach their "aha moment" within the first week? (0 = nobody gets onboarded, 10 = every new member completes a 14-day sequence) | |
| 3 | **Retention Rate** — What % of members who joined 30 days ago are still active? (0 = <20%, 5 = ~50%, 10 = 70%+) | |
| 4 | **Content Consistency** — Do you post scheduled content on a predictable weekly rhythm? (0 = random posting, 10 = daily themes executed every week) | |
| 5 | **Moderation Quality** — Are community guidelines enforced consistently and fairly? (0 = chaos, 10 = clear rules + consistent enforcement) | |
| 6 | **Growth Engine** — Are you growing through referrals, partnerships, or organic channels? (0 = no growth system, 10 = 3+ acquisition channels producing steady growth) | |
| 7 | **Tier Structure** — Do you have distinct tiers or roles for different member levels? (0 = flat, everyone treated the same, 10 = clear free/paid/VIP tiers) | |
| 8 | **Member Recognition** — Do you regularly spotlight member contributions and wins? (0 = never, 10 = weekly member spotlights with genuine recognition) | |
| 9 | **Feedback Loop** — Do you collect and act on member feedback? (0 = never ask, 10 = structured feedback system with visible action taken) | |
| 10 | **Community Culture** — Is your server welcoming, inclusive, and self-sustaining? (0 = toxic, 10 = positive culture with member-led initiatives) | |

---

### Scoring

| Total Score | Rating | What It Means |
|------------|--------|---------------|
| 80-100 | Thriving | Your community is healthy, sustainable, and growing. Focus on scaling systems and developing champions. |
| 60-79 | Solid | Good foundation but room to grow. Pick your lowest-scoring areas and build systems there. |
| 40-59 | Developing | You have members but lack systems. Focus on onboarding, content consistency, and engagement tracking. |
| 20-39 | Fragile | Your community needs structural work. Start with the Community Launch Checklist and build from there. |
| 0-19 | Just Starting | You're at the beginning — that's okay! Focus on purpose, platform setup, and your first 10 members. |
