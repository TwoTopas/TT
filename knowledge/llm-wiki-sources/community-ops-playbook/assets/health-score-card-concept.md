# Health Score Card — Social Media Graphic Concept

## Purpose

A branded social media graphic (1200 × 628 px) that users generate after completing the Community Health Score quiz. They share it on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, or Discord to show "My Discord Community Health Score: XX/100" — making the score visible and driving word-of-mouth for the playbook.

---

## Dimensions

- **1200 × 628 px** (standard social media / Open Graph ratio)
- Export as PNG with transparent or solid background

---

## Color Scheme

| Element | Hex | Usage |
|---------|-----|-------|
| Navy background | `#1F4E79` | Full card background, solid or subtle gradient |
| Green accent | `#00B050` | Score number, divider lines, CTA button |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary text |
| Light gray | `#D0D5DD` | Secondary text, labels |
| Dark teal (optional) | `#0D3B5C` | Card inner shadow or border |

---

## Layout (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Top Bar — Brand Header
- **Left:** Small gear icon (16px, white, 24×24 bounding box)
- **Text:** "THE DISCORD COMMUNITY OPERATIONS PLAYBOOK" in all-caps, white, 10pt bold, tracked +2
- **Right:** Divider line (green, 1px)

### 2. Hero — Score Display
- **Label:** "My Discord Community Health Score" in white, 28pt semibold, centered
- **Score:** Dynamic "XX/100" in green (`#00B050`), 72pt bold, centered
- **Rating badge:** Below the score, a small pill badge — "Thriving" / "Solid" / "Developing" / "Fragile" / "Just Starting" — white text on navy background with green border, 16pt medium

### 3. Breakdown — Category Dots
- 10 small dots in a horizontal row (centered)
- Each dot represents one of the 10 assessment categories
- Filled green (`#00B050`) = scored high (7-10), filled navy (`#1F4E79` with white border) = medium (4-6), hollow (white border on navy) = low (0-3)
- Below the dots, a small label: "10-Point Assessment" in light gray, 9pt

### 4. CTA / Share Prompt
- **Text:** "Take your free assessment at discordopsplaybook.com" in white, 12pt regular
- **Divider line** (green, 1px)
- **Small footer text:** "Built with The Discord Community Operations Playbook" in light gray, 8pt

## Export Instructions for Designers

1. **Canvas:** 1200 × 628 px at 72 DPI (web resolution)
2. **Background:** Solid navy `#1F4E79` or subtle top-to-bottom gradient from `#1F4E79` to `#0D3B5C`
3. **Font:** Use Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans (open-source) — or fall back to Helvetica Neue / Arial
4. **Score number:** Green `#00B050`, bold, 72pt — make it the clear focal point
5. **Dots:** 10 circles, 20×20 px each, spaced 28px apart center-to-center
6. **Final export:** PNG with flattened layers — ready for upload

## Why This Works (Contagious Mechanics)

- **Social Currency:** A score is a status signal — people compare and compete
- **Public:** The branded card makes the playbook visible every time someone shares
- **Triggers:** Seeing the card in a feed reminds people to take their own assessment
- **Practical Value:** The 10-category breakdown gives actionable insight, not just a vanity score
