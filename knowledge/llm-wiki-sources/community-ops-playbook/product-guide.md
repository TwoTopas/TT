## 3. Platform-Specific Guidance

### For Discord Communities
- Structure: channel categories (Welcome, Community, Resources, Events, Voice)
- Use threads for focused discussions
- Bot recommendations: MEE6, Carl-bot, Dyno for moderation and engagement
- Role-based access for tiered communities

### For Circle Communities
- Spaces for topic-based discussions
- Course integration for paid tiers
- Live streams and Q&A sessions
- Member profiles and direct messaging

### For Skool Communities
- Gamification: leaderboards, levels, achievements
- Calendar-based course delivery
- Classroom-style discussions
- Native monetization ($9/mo platform fee)

### For Email/Newsletter Communities (Substack)
- Welcome sequence: 3-5 emails over 14 days
- Community as a perk: paid subscribers get access
- Weekly digest as the primary touchpoint
