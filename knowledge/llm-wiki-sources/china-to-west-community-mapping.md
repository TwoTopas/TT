## Key Structural Adaptations Needed

### 1. Daily Schedule → Weekly Rhythm
Chinese model: Hour-by-hour daily schedule for WeChat group
Western adaptation: Weekly content calendar for Discord/Circle with daily themes

### 2. 个人号 (Personal Account) → Brand Voice Guidelines
Chinese: One persona managing all 1-on-1 chats
Western: Community manager persona + automated onboarding sequences

### 3. Tiered Groups
Chinese: 鱼塘群 (fishpond) → 中转群 (transit) → 核心群 (core)
Western: Free trial → Paid member → VIP/Annual tier

### 4. Seeding (托) → Strategic Engagement
Chinese: Hired "seeds" in group to drive conversations
Western: Community champions / Power users / Moderator programs

### 5. Monetization Funnel
Chinese: Group → flash sale → paid course → membership
Western: Free content → paid community → courses → coaching

## Style Guidelines
- Use "you" and direct address (not corporate "we")
- Short sentences, bullet points, action items
- Platform-specific examples (Discord channels, Circle spaces)
- No Chinese analogies (no WeChat/微信 references)
- No translation-ese — rewrite concepts in native English
- Use Western metrics: NPS, DAU/MAU, churn rate, activation rate
