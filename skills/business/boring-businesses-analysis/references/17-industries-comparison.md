## Key Finding: Service Frequency as Demand Predictor

The single best leading indicator for software demand is **service frequency**.

| Frequency | Software Need | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Weekly/Monthly | **High** (manual is painful) | Pool cleaning, pest control |
| Quarterly | **Medium-High** | Window cleaning, landscaping |
| Yearly | **Low-Medium** | Chimney sweep, carpet cleaning |
| 2-5 years | **Very Low** | Septic pumping (notebook works) |

## Bottom of the funnel: Etsy Digital Products
