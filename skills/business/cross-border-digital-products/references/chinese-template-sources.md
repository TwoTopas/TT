# China-Specific Template Sources for Digital Products

## Where to find source materials

### 百度文库 (Baidu Wenku)
- URL: wenku.baidu.com
- What: Free/paid document library with massive template collection
- Search terms: 客户管理表, CRM表格, 销售管理表, 私域运营SOP
- Quality: Wide range (¥0-¥30), many free ones available
- Limits: Some require Baidu download credits (下载券)
- Tool: browser tool may fail due to GBK encoding; use ddgs search to find content summaries

### 淘宝/天猫 (Taobao/Tmall)
- URL: taobao.com
- What: Paid templates by Chinese creators
- Search terms: Excel模板 客户管理, 私域CRM模板, Notion模板
- Price range: ¥5-¥50
- Quality: Generally more polished than Baidu Wenku, often includes usage instructions
- Extraction method: Search via ddgs first (`site:taobao.com ...`), but most product pages require login or CAPTCHA. Browser tool may be blocked. Use search results for pricing and feature data.

### 飞书模板中心 (Feishu/Lark Template Center)
- URL: feishu.cn/templates (or feishu.cn in Chinese mode)
- What: Free multi-dimensional tables (多维表格), project management
- Advantage: Modern, clean design, free to use and export
- Search: 飞书模板 CRM, 飞书 客户管理
- Export: Can export to Excel format, then convert to Google Sheets

### 知乎专栏 (Zhihu Articles)
- What: In-depth methodology articles
- Search pattern: `site:zhihu.com 私域运营 方法论`
- Content: Full explanations of Chinese private domain CRM techniques
- Not directly downloadable, but provides methodology to rebuild

### 小红书 (Xiaohongshu / RED)
- What: Short-form content sharing, trending methodologies
- Search pattern: `site:xiaohongshu.com 私域 模板`
- Character: Visual-first, good for understanding current trends
- Harder to extract structured data; use for trend awareness only

## Platform Access Notes
- **Most Chinese platforms block automated scraping** — rely on ddgs search results for structure and pricing
- **Encoding issues**: GBK/GB2312 sites may render as garbage in browser tool
- **Mobile-only platforms** (小红书) may redirect to app download instead of web content
- **Best approach**: Skim via ddgs search, download actual files manually, send to AI for processing
