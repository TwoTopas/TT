## Chinese Seller Registration Guide

### Gumroad ✅ (works)
1. Sign up at gumroad.com with any email
2. No verification documents required
3. Set payout to **PayPal** (works with Chinese PayPal accounts)
4. PayPal → Chinese bank card withdrawal (fees apply)
5. No US entity, no LLC, no tax forms needed upfront

### Etsy ❌ (blocked)
- Chinese mainland sellers cannot open new shops since 2021
- The Payoneer loophole (registering via Payoneer US bank account) was closed June 2026
- Only existing Chinese shops can stay open

### Platform comparison

| Aspect | Gumroad | Etsy |
|--------|---------|------|
| Signup for Chinese | ✅ Easy | ❌ Blocked |
| Audience built-in | 🟡 Some (Discover) | ✅ Yes (marketplace) |
| Fee | 10% | $0.20 + 6.5% |
| Avg price point | $30-49 | $5-20 |
| Best for | Niche tools, higher-priced | High-volume, trendy |
