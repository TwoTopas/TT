# Digital Product EULA Template

A complete LICENSE.txt template for Gumroad ZIP download products. Copy into your ZIP root directory before listing.

## Instructions

1. Replace `[Product Name]` with your actual product name
2. Replace `[Topic]` with your product category (e.g. "community management", "spreadsheet templates")
3. Replace `[Date]` with today's date
4. Fill in tier-specific license terms in Section 2
5. Choose governing law in Section 9 (Delaware recommended for US-facing products)
