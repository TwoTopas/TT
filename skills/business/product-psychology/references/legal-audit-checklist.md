# Legal Audit Checklist — Digital Products (Gumroad)

Pre-launch checklist for selling digital products (templates, playbooks, tools) on Gumroad from China to US market.

## 🔴 Pre-Launch (Must Fix Before Publishing)

### FTC Testimonials Rule
- [ ] Are any testimonials fabricated? → Add footnote: `*Names and identifying details have been changed. Testimonials are illustrative composites based on real outcomes.*`
- [ ] Does the footnote appear on EVERY page where testimonials appear? (listing, README, quick-start guide, all files)
- [ ] No specific unverifiable numbers in testimonials (member counts, revenue, etc.)

### Trademark in Product Title
- [ ] Product title does NOT start with a third-party trademark
- [ ] Format: `The [Product Name] (for [Platform] Servers)` not `The [Platform] [Product Name]`
- [ ] "Works with [Platform]" is fine in the description, NOT in the title
- [ ] All platform mentions are descriptive, not endorsement-implying

### Trademark Disclaimers
- [ ] Every file that mentions a platform name has the disclaimer:
  > `*[Platform] is a trademark of [Company]. This product is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by [Company].*`
- [ ] Files to check: listing page, ZIP README, quick-start guide, playbook appendix, HTML assets

### General Disclaimer
- [ ] Listing includes: `This product provides educational information and templates. Results are not guaranteed... Use at your own risk.`  
- [ ] ZIP README includes same disclaimer
- [ ] Quick-start guide includes same disclaimer
- [ ] Playbook/appendix includes same disclaimer

### Factual Claims
- [ ] No "Join 500+" unless verifiable → use "Join hundreds" or "Join many"
- [ ] No unsubstantiated superlatives ("#1", "the best", "most popular" without data)
- [ ] No fake scarcity (countdown timers, "limited" that aren't real)

### Refund Policy
- [ ] Listing states policy clearly (No Refunds or 30-Day)
- [ ] EULA Section 8 matches listing
- [ ] Gumroad product settings match (set in dashboard)
- [ ] If "No Refunds": logic explained (`Due to the digital nature...`)

## 🟡 Important (Complete Before Launch)

### EULA / LICENSE.txt
- [ ] LICENSE.txt in ZIP root directory
- [ ] Covers: license grant by tier, restrictions, IP ownership, disclaimer, liability cap, governing law, refund policy
- [ ] Agency tier terms explicitly defined (what IS vs IS NOT allowed)
- [ ] Governing law specified (Delaware recommended)

### Agency/Commercial License Terms
- [ ] ✅ What licensees CAN do (clearly listed)
- [ ] ❌ What licensees CANNOT do (clearly listed)
- [ ] Scope unambiguous: per-project vs unlimited, attribution needed vs white-label

### Platform Compliance
- [ ] Check Gumroad prohibited items list (https://gumroad.com/prohibited)
- [ ] Not reselling PLR (private label rights) content
- [ ] Not OEM or copyrighted media
- [ ] Not soliciting/consulting services

## 🟢 Cross-Border (China → US)

- [ ] Gumroad account registered (email + PayPal — China allowed)
- [ ] W-8BEN filed if applicable (US tax treaty: 10% withholding)
- [ ] US LLC considered if monthly revenue >$500
- [ ] Payment in US dollars, pricing in $USD
- [ ] Content in native English, all dates MM/DD/YYYY, currency $

## Reference Sources

- FTC Consumer Reviews Rule: 16 CFR Part 465 (effective Oct 2024)
- Gumroad Terms of Service: https://gumroad.com/terms
- Gumroad Prohibited Products: https://gumroad.com/prohibited
- Discord Brand Guidelines: https://discord.com/branding
- EU Consumer Rights Directive (CRD): 14-day withdrawal right, digital content waiver
