# CONTAGIOUS STEPPS Scoring Guide

Full methodology for scoring product virality using Jonah Berger's STEPPS framework.

## Scoring Protocol

1. Read the product files (README, listing page, actual content/templates)
2. Score each dimension 0-10 with EVIDENCE from the actual files
3. Total = sum of 6 dimensions (max 60)
4. Target: 48+/60 for "ready to launch"

## Dimension Details

### S — Social Currency (0-10)
Does sharing this product make people look good?

**Evidence to look for:**
- Status signaling (price tiers, "pro" positioning)
- Insider knowledge or secrets angle
- Social proof counters ("Join hundreds of operators" — avoid specific numbers without evidence)
- Shareable outputs (scores, badges, certificates)
- Testimonials with identifiable user tiers

**Weak signals (score 0-4):** No sharing mechanism, no status angle, pure utility
**Strong signals (score 7-10):** Visual share card, buyer badge, leaderboard, tier-based status

### T — Triggers (0-10)
What everyday cues remind people of the product?

**Evidence to look for:**
- Environmental triggers (tied to frequent activities: "every time you open Discord")
- Recurring use patterns (weekly content calendar, KPI dashboard)
- Email/DM re-engagement sequences
- Cross-platform triggers

**Weak signals:** Single trigger, context-limited
**Strong signals:** Multiple triggers across platforms, recurring use built into product

### E — Emotion (0-10)
Does it evoke high-arousal feelings?

**Two distinct emotional modes (do not conflate):**

**Mode 1: Excitement-based** (awe, excitement, amusement, anger, outrage)
- Works for: consumer apps, media, entertainment, dramatic transformations
- Signals: "couldn't believe it worked," awe-inspiring numbers, emotional rollercoaster
- Not achievable for: template/playbook/tool products

**Mode 2: Anxiety-relief-based** (tension → release, dread → peace, guilt → quiet confidence)
- Works for: templates, playbooks, tools, SOPs, productivity systems, operations products
- **The primary emotional lever for practical/business products.**
- Anxiety → relief is a HIGH-arousal arc because the release of accumulated tension is physiologically acute. Do NOT score it as "low-arousal" just because the outcome is calmness.

**Evidence to look for (anxiety-relief mode):**

| Level | Signal |
|-------|--------|
| Frame | Pain not as "problem" but as "the feeling" — visceral description of the anxiety state |
| Physical anchors | Sleep disruption ("2 AM", "checking before bed"), bodily sensations ("knot in stomach", "weight on chest"), behavior patterns ("checking while brushing teeth") |
| Relief specifics | "Peace of mind", "quiet confidence", "first time I didn't feel it", "sleep through the night" |
| Product content | Actual product material that delivers on the emotional promise (burnout guide, anxiety inventory, shutdown ritual) |
| Shareable bonus | Extra assessment embedded in the product (burnout risk score, stress inventory) that generates shareable output |
| Closing frame | Before/after not just of metrics but of daily feeling ("before: dread at 11 PM → after: Friday evening without guilt") |

**Weak signals (score 0-4):** Purely practical/instructional, no emotional arc, problem framed as logistical not emotional
**Medium signals (score 5-6):** Pain→solution narrative but no physical/visceral language, testimonials about utility not feeling
**Strong signals (score 7-10):** Multiple physical sensation anchors, named customer describing relief not just results, product content reinforces emotional promise, bonus anxiety-relief assessment

**Anxiety-Relief Fix Playbook:**

| Lever | Technique | Example |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Lead narrative | Start with "the feeling" not "the problem" | "11 PM, knot in your stomach, scrolling one last time" not "your community needs better ops" |
| Testimonials | Rewrite for emotional payload, not just metrics | "Peace of mind alone was worth the price" not "saved me 10 hours" |
| Product content | Embed relief in the product itself | Add Burnout Prevention section, Anxiety Inventory 5-question check, Friday Shutdown Ritual |
| Bonus shareable | Create a separate assessment within the product | Burnout Risk Score (5 questions) as bonus content with its own share card |
| Closing frame | End on the emotional outcome, not just the offer | "Stop winging it. Start sleeping." |

**Preference order when scoring Emotion:**
1. Identify which emotional mode fits the product (most templates/playbooks = anxiety-relief)
2. Score on that mode's signals only — don't penalize for lacking excitement signals
3. Apply fixes that deliver on the emotional arc in the PRODUCT not just the listing

### P — Public (0-10)
Can others see people using it?

**Evidence to look for:**
- Visible usage signals (badges, watermarks, profile additions)
- Shareable outputs (auto-generated cards, reports)
- Social media sharing buttons/pre-written posts
- Buyers-only community where members see each other
- "Powered by" branding on outputs

**Weak signals:** Purely private (ZIP download, PDF), no visible artifacts
**Strong signals:** Auto-generated visual cards, badge for profiles, public leaderboard

### P — Practical Value (0-10)
Is it useful enough to pass along?

**Evidence to look for:**
- Pre-filled templates vs empty ones
- Ready-to-use scripts/copy
- Time-savings claims with specific numbers
- Forward/send-to-friend CTAs
- Free sample or lead magnet

**Weak signals:** Empty templates, theory-only, no time savings
**Strong signals:** Fill-in-the-blank templates, pre-written copy, specific hour savings

### S — Stories (0-10)
Is it wrapped in a retellable narrative?

**Evidence to look for:**
- Origin story (founder's journey)
- Customer transformation stories with named characters
- "Tell a friend" templates
- Narrative arc: problem → solution → transformation
- Surprising or remarkable details

**Weak signals:** No stories, generic testimonials without names
**Strong signals:** Named customer with specific numbers, retellable in 30 seconds

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0-3 | Dimension not activated |
| 4-6 | Partially activated, room for improvement |
| 7-9 | Strongly activated, real sharing driver |
| 10 | Perfectly engineered |

## Interpreting Results

| Total | Verdict |
|-------|---------|
| 48-60 | ✅ Ready to launch |
| 36-47 | 🟡 Needs work — apply top 3 recomended fixes |
| 0-35 | 🔴 Major gaps — redesign before launch |

## Common Fixes by Weak Dimension

| Weak Dimension | Best Fix |
|----------------|----------|
| Social Currency <5 | Add shareable score/quiz output, buyer badge, social proof counter |
| Triggers <5 | Create environmental hook, add email/DM re-engagement sequence |
| Emotion (excitement) <5 | Add dramatic before/after, awe-inspiring numbers, named case study |
| Emotion (anxiety-relief) <5 | Apply 5-layer stack: physical anchors → emotional testimonials → product relief → bonus assessment → closing frame (see Anxiety-Relief Playbook) |
| Public <5 | Build auto-generated visual card, add "Built with" badge, create owners-only community |
| Practical Value <5 | Pre-fill all templates, add specific time-savings claims, create free sample |
| Stories <5 | Write origin story, create customer transformation template, add named case study |
