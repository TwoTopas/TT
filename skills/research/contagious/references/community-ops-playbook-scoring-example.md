## Key Lessons

- **Practical Value is the easiest dimension to max out** for template/content products
- **Public is the hardest** — digital downloads are inherently invisible. Fix: create shareable artifacts (score cards, badges, watermarks)
- **Anxiety-relief is the correct emotional mode for tool/playbook products.** Do NOT try to score on excitement — score on the tension→release arc. Physical sensation anchors (sleep disruption, bodily tension, compulsive checking) are the strongest signal.
- **The emotional arc must exist in the product content, not just the marketing.** Adding a Burnout Prevention section and Anxiety Inventory to the playbook itself makes the relief promise real — customers reading the product feel the relief while using it.
- **Scoring is subjective between runs** — the same product scored 34, 42, and 42 by different assessors. Focus on the fix recommendations, not the number.
- **The improvement loop is what creates value**, not the initial score
