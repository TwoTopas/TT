# Digital Product Platform Accessibility — Chinese Creator Perspective

> Research date: 2026-06
> Context: Chinese (mainland) creator, no US LLC, no US bank account.
> Goal: Sell digital products/ebooks to international audience, get paid to China.

## Platform Matrix

| Platform | Chinese Reg | Payout | Rate | Traffic | Verdict |
|----------|-------------|--------|------|---------|---------|
| **Gumroad** | ✅ Email only | PayPal | 10%+fees | Built-in Discover | Best for ebook/tool MVP |
| **Ko-fi** | ✅ Email only | PayPal | 0-5% | None (link sharing) | Best for low-fee backup |
| **Payhip** | ✅ Email only | PayPal | 0-5% | None | Clean UX, growing |
| **Lemon Squeezy** | ✅ Email only | PayPal/Bank | 5%+$0.50 | None | MoR handles tax |
| **Amazon KDP** | ✅ Address + W-8BEN | Payoneer | 30-65% | Amazon search | Long tail passive |
| **Notion Market** | ✅ | Stripe | ~5% | Built-in | Templates only |
| **Etsy** | ❌ Blocked 2021+ | — | — | — | Do not attempt |
| **Shopify/Stripe** | ❌ Needs US LLC | — | — | — | Needs US entity |

## Payment Pipeline (China)

Platform → PayPal/Payoneer → China bank card (USD→CNY)

- PayPal supports Chinese bank card withdrawals (~2.5% below market rate)
- Payoneer: used for Amazon KDP payouts
- No need for Stripe or US bank account

## Multi-Platform Distribution Strategy

Launch → Gumroad ($29 full price)
       → Ko-fi ($29 backup, lower fees)
       → Amazon KDP ($9.99 Kindle, months later for long-tail)

## Key Failure Patterns

1. Etsy is dead for Chinese sellers (2021+) — Payoneer workaround patched 2026-06.
2. Stripe-requiring platforms are dead — Stripe does not support China mainland accounts.
3. "Supports PayPal" is sufficient — PayPal-to-China-bank-card is the viable pipeline.
4. Cross-list across platforms — Gumroad discoverability is modest.
