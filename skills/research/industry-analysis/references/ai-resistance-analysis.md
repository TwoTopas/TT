# AI Resistance Analysis

When evaluating a digital product or SaaS direction, assess how likely AI is to commoditize or eliminate the product within 6-24 months.

## Evaluation dimensions

| Resistance level | Description | Examples |
|-----------------|-------------|----------|
| 🔴 **Low** (months) | AI can generate a passable version today | Wall art, basic planners, simple workbooks, AI prompt packs |
| 🟡 **Medium** (1-2 years) | AI can assist but human quality/judgment still matters | Social media templates, Etsy SEO tools, basic Canva templates |
| ✅ **High** (2+ years) | Requires domain knowledge, complex logic, multi-tool integration, or real-world operational experience | Complex Notion systems, automated Excel/Sheets with scripts, industry-specific CRM workflows, multi-tool automation pipelines |

## Assessment framework

1. **Can AI generate a prototype in <30 seconds?** → Low resistance
   - e.g., "create a printable wall art with lavender fields and a motivational quote"
2. **Does the product require complex conditional logic?** → High resistance
   - e.g., nested IF formulas in Sheets, multi-database Notion rollups with formulas
3. **Does the product require multi-tool integration?** → High resistance
   - e.g., Notion + Google Sheets + Zapier + Gmail automation
4. **Does the product embed industry-specific knowledge?** → High resistance
   - e.g., "knowing that septic inspections require a 5-field record with tank type, gallons, and disposal manifest"
5. **Is the product purely visual/design?** → Low-to-medium resistance
   - AI image generation improves quarterly
6. **Does the product require "taste" or brand consistency?** → Medium resistance
   - AI can generate single assets but struggles with multi-asset brand consistency

## Practical application

When choosing between product directions:

```
High demand + Low AI resistance = Short-term opportunity (6-12 months max)
High demand + High AI resistance = Sustainable business (2+ years)
Medium demand + High AI resistance = Niche play (small but defensible)
Low demand + anything = Skip
```

## Case study: Etsy digital products

| Product type | AI resistance | Why |
|-------------|---------------|-----|
| Printable wall art | 🔴 Low | Midjourney/DALL-E generates immediately |
| Digital planners | 🔴 Low | AI can generate layouts + content in seconds |
| Simple workbooks | 🔴 Low | GPT writes workbook content instantly |
| AI prompt packs | 🔴 Low | AI itself writes the best prompts |
| Canva templates | 🟡 Medium | Canva itself is embedding AI generation |
| Social media templates | 🟡 Medium | Style consistency still matters |
| Complex Notion systems | ✅ High | Multi-database logic + formulas + rollups |
| Excel/Sheets with scripts | ✅ High | Conditional logic + Apps Script automation |
| Industry-specific CRM | ✅ High | Requires domain knowledge to design |
