2. **Septic Tech Platform** ($9–12B, 70K+ operators, mission-critical recurring)
3. **Pool Maintenance App** ($8–12B, 15K+ operators, weekly recurring, wealthy customers)
4. **HVAC Micro-SaaS** ($130B+, 120K+ operators, but more competitive software)

### Tier 2: Niche Domination
5. **Pressure Washing + Window Cleaning Quote Engine** — AI photo → instant quote
6. **Chimney Sweep Booking Platform** — Tiny but 0% tech, annual mandatory = recurring
7. **Grease Trap Route Compliance** — Regulatory tracking + waste manifest

### AI Angles by Industry
- **Pest Control:** Computer vision pest ID → automated treatment recommendations
- **Septic:** Predictive pump scheduling, compliance alerts, digital logbook
- **Pool:** Chemical balance prediction, automated customer reports
- **Pressure Washing:** AI photo analysis for instant quoting, social media auto-posts
- **Chimney:** Weather-aware annual reminders, CSIA cert management
