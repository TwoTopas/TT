# Pest Control SaaS Competitive Landscape — June 2026

## Market
- 30-35K operators in US, service frequency: quarterly/monthly
- Software penetration ~15-20% (higher than septic but lower than HVAC)
- Willingness to pay: $50-100/mo (higher than septic's $20-30)

## Competitor pricing

| Product | Starting Price | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| GorillaDesk | $49/mo | Basic plan, cheapest pest-specific option |
| PestPac (WorkWave) | $125-200+/mo | Enterprise, too expensive for solo |
| FieldRoutes (ServiceTitan) | $125-200+/mo | Mid-market, quote-based |
| Jobber | $49/mo | Generalist, not pest-specific |
| HouseCall Pro | $59/mo | Generalist, lacks chemical tracking |
| FieldSync | $9.99 CAD | Newly built by a Redditor for his father-in-law |
| Briostack | ~$50/mo | Pest-specific, newer entrant |

## Key gap
Lightweight chemical tracking + quarterly auto-renewal + SMS reminders for solo operators at $29-49/mo.
