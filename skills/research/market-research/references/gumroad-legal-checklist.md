# Gumroad Legal Review Checklist

> Captured from a 2026-06-10 session: 法务审核 of a community ops playbook sold as ZIP download on Gumroad.
> Apply to any Gumroad digital product before launch.

## 1. Testimonials — FTC Consumer Reviews Rule

**Effective Oct 2024.** The FTC Rule on Consumer Reviews and Testimonials bans fabricated reviews with civil penalties.

| Risk Level | Situation | Fix |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| 🔴 HIGH | Named testimonials (Sarah K., ~200 members) that are fabricated | A. Anonymize (remove full name + specific numbers), or B. Add disclaimer: *"Names and identifying details have been changed. Testimonials are illustrative composites."* |
| 🟡 MEDIUM | Fabricated testimonials without names | Same fix — any fictional review needs disclaimer |
| 🟢 SAFE | Real customer testimonials with permission | Document permission. No fix needed. |

## 2. Trademark Mentions in Product Name

| Risk Level | Situation | Fix |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| 🔴 HIGH | Product name starts with trademark ("The Discord Community Operations Playbook") | Add disclaimer: *"Discord is a trademark of Discord Inc. This product is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Discord Inc."* or move platform name to subtitle: *"The Community Operations Playbook (for Discord Servers)"* |
| 🟡 MEDIUM | Describing compatibility ("Works with Discord, Circle, Skool") | Safe as descriptive use — no fix needed |

## 3. Disclaimer of Liability

If the product provides advice, templates, or methodologies:

```
Disclaimer: This product provides educational information and templates for
[domain]. Results are not guaranteed. The author makes no representations or
warranties regarding outcomes. Use at your own risk. This is not legal or
financial advice.
```

Include in:
- [ ] Gumroad listing page (bottom, before footer)
- [ ] ZIP README (first file user sees)
- [ ] Any guide or playbook within the product

## 4. Commercial License Terms (Agency tier)

If offering commercial/white-label licensing, define scope explicitly:

```
Agency License Terms:
- ✅ Use templates for unlimited client communities
- ✅ Modify and rebrand as your own
- ❌ Cannot resell original unmodified templates as a standalone product
- ❌ Cannot claim original authorship of the playbook content
```

Place these in the listing near the Agency pricing section.

## 5. Refund Policy

Gumroad default is seller-managed refunds. Being explicit builds trust:

```
30-Day Refund: If [product] doesn't help you [outcome], email within 30 days
for a full refund. No questions asked.
```

## 6. Gumroad Prohibited Items Check

Our product category (templates, playbooks, tools, spreadsheets) is NOT on the prohibited list. Verify if your product type falls under any of these banned categories:
- Consulting firms (prohibited)
- Services / business opportunities (prohibited — but templates are products, not services)
- Copyrighted media / unauthorized software
- Essay mills / paper mills

**Key distinction:** Templates, spreadsheets, and informational playbooks are PRODUCTS not SERVICES. Gumroad's ban on "services" and "consulting firms" applies to ongoing service delivery, not downloadable information products.

## 7. Data Privacy

- Gumroad handles payment data and is the merchant of record
- If you collect emails via free lead magnet: include a privacy notice in the listing
- GDPR applies if marketing to EU customers — Gumroad's privacy policy covers transactional data
- Health Score Tool with localStorage: no data leaves the user's browser, no privacy issue

## Full Audit Checklist

- [ ] Testimonials: real OR properly disclaimed
- [ ] Trademark: disclaimer present if trademark in product name
- [ ] Liability: disclaimer in listing AND inside product ZIP
- [ ] Commercial license: terms clearly scoped
- [ ] Refund policy: stated in listing
- [ ] Gumroad prohibited items: verified your product is not on the list
- [ ] Privacy: no unnecessary data collection
