# Western Community Platform Reference
## Platform mapping for China→English info arbitrage products

## Platform Equivalents

| Chinese Platform | Category | Western Equivalent | Key Difference |
|-----------------|----------|-------------------|----------------|
| 抖音 (Douyin) | Short-form Video | **TikTok** (1.59B MAU, 4.2% engagement) | Same product family. Algorithm shifted to community-aligned content (2026). |
| B站 (Bilibili) | Long-form Video | **YouTube** (2.5B+ MAU) | B站 has strong community culture (弹幕). YouTube is algorithm-driven, less peer-to-peer. |
| 小红书 (Xiaohongshu) | Lifestyle/Discovery | **Instagram + Pinterest** mix | Instagram: 2B MAU, ~0.6% organic engagement. No exact "grass-planting" (种草) culture equivalent. |
| 微信群 | Messaging Community | **Discord** (200M MAU, 94 min/day) | Key difference: WeChat = chat-first, Discord = channel-first. Discord has 19M+ active servers. |
| 企业微信 | Business Comms | **Slack** (65M+ DAU) | Slack is channel-first with deep integrations. |
| 公众号 | Long-form Content | **Substack** / Newsletter | Substack: 3M+ paid subscriptions, 5-10% free→paid conversion. |
| 微博 (Weibo) | Microblogging | **X (Twitter)** (550M+ MAU) | Real-time, news-driven, thread-based. |
| 知乎 (Zhihu) | Q&A / Knowledge | **Reddit** (1.1B+ MAU, 100K+ subreddits) | Both are interest-based, not follower-based. |
| 视频号 | In-app Short Video | **Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts** | Embedded in existing ecosystem. |

## Western Community Lifecycle

```
Discovery → Onboarding → Activation → Engagement → Retention → Advocacy
```

**Key difference from Chinese model:**
- Western: content-first, then community (join for content, stay for connection)
- Chinese: relationship-first, then content (join for guanxi, content is secondary)
- Western onboarding: 3-7 email sequence + in-platform guided tour
- Chinese onboarding: red packet + group rules + immediate personal follow
