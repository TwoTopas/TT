# Three-Direction Comparison — June 2026

- Code 96% ready, but **paused due to weak demand signal**
- 2-5 year service cycle → notebook adequate → no software need
- Key lesson: market size ≠ market demand; validate willingness to change before building

## Pest Control SaaS

- Quarterly frequency → real need for reminders/scheduling
- Existing tools $125-200+ → gap at $29-49 for solo operators
- Risk: same US company/tax/compliance barriers as SepticSaver ($400-700/yr, IRS Form 5472)
