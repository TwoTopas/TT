# Etsy Digital Products — Landscape Summary

> Condensed from June 2026 research. Full report in knowledge base: `06-机会扫描/etsy-digital-products-deep-research.md`

## Key questions answered

### Is it profitable?
- **Most sellers**: $200–1,000/mo (3–12 months in)
- **Good sellers**: $1,000–3,000/mo (6–12 months, daily work)
- **Top sellers**: $3,000–15,000+/mo (1+ year, multi-platform)
- **Outliers** (PsychicGoddess1): $15K–33K/mo, 16K reviews at 4.9★
- Margins: 85–92% after Etsy fees

### Does it require a US company?
**No.** Etsy handles all payments. You can sell as an individual, no LLC needed. Chinese sellers operate successfully on Etsy using PayPal for withdrawals.

### Key seller pain points (from Reddit)

1. **Etsy fees are 12–20%** — listing $0.20 + 6.5% transaction + 3% payment + optional 15% offsite ads
2. **SEO is hard** — new shops get no organic traffic for weeks
3. **Must add listings daily** — not passive income, requires consistent work
4. **Competition saturating** — easy entry means crowded categories

### Buyer pain points
- Can't download from mobile app (web only)
- File format incompatibility
- Can't return digital purchases

### Chinese seller considerations
- Etsy accepts Chinese sellers (ID + address verification)
- PayPal withdrawal works
- No US tax ID / ITIN required for non-US residents
- Account verification can be triggered by rapid growth

### Revenue estimation formula (from reviews)

```
lifetime orders ≈ reviews × 10–20
revenue = lifetime orders × avg order value ÷ years active
```
