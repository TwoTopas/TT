How This Funnel Works

Instagram Stories work differently from feed posts. Feed is for discovery. Stories are for conversion. This funnel turns casual story viewers into people who take action.

4 stages. One story at a time. Each stage has a specific job.

Stage 1: Attention (Hook Them)

You have 3 seconds. If the first story doesn't stop them from swiping, the rest doesn't matter.

TemplateCaptionVisual
POV hook"POV: You just found the exact strategy your small biz needs."Video of you pointing at text
Question poll"Quick yes or no: Do you post stories every day?" [Yes/No poll]Text on plain background
Bold statement"Most people use Stories wrong. Here is the fix."Big text, minimal design
Reveal teaser"I tested 4 story strategies for 30 days. Results are surprising."Countdown sticker

Stage 2: Interest (Build Curiosity)

They swiped past the hook. Now give them a reason to stay. Share something useful.

TemplateCaptionVisual
Quick tip"One thing that doubled my story engagement: add a question sticker every 3 stories."Screenshot of your analytics
Mini case study"I posted 1 story every day for a week. Here is what happened: 2x profile visits, 3 DMs."Numbers on screen
Behind the scenes"This is how I plan my content week. Three columns: Hook, Value, CTA."Photo of planner

Stage 3: Desire (Show the Path)

Now they know you have something valuable. Show them the next step clearly.

TemplateCaptionVisual
Problem-solution"Still posting random stories and hoping? There is a better way. Swipe up for the template."Before/after split
Feature highlight"This strategy works for 3 types of businesses: coaches, ecommerce, local services."Text list
Social proof"Just got a DM: 'I tried your strategy and got 3 leads in one day.'"Screenshot of DM

Stage 4: Action (Close)

This is where you ask. Be direct. No vague "link in bio." Tell them exactly what to do.
