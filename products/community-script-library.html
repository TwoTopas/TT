30-Day Community Script Library

Daily engagement prompts, welcome messages, and retention scripts for any community platform

How to Use This Library

Each day has a specific script. Post it in your community (Discord, Circle, Slack, Skool) at the listed time. Mix and match based on your community's size and activity level.

Script Categories

TypeGoalBest For
WelcomeOnboard new membersDay 1-5, first-time joiners
DiscussionSpark conversationMid-week engagement
ValueShare resource/tipMaintaining reason to stay
ActivityLow-friction participationLurkers, quiet members
SocialBuild connectionsCommunity bonding
FeedbackCollect inputProduct/community improvement
CelebrationRecognize membersMilestones, wins

The Scripts

DAY 1 Welcome
Theme: First impression
👋 Hey everyone! Welcome to [community name]. Quick intro from me: I built this space for [audience] who [pain point]. My goal is to make this the most useful [topic] community on the internet. Now it's your turn. Drop a comment below: - What's your name? - What brought you here? - What's one thing you want to learn this month? Go!
Platform: #welcome channel · Best time: Morning
DAY 2 Discussion
Theme: Icebreaker
Quick question for everyone: What is the ONE tool or habit that changed how you work this year? Could be a software tool, a daily routine, a mindset shift. Just pick one and share. I will go first: [your example]. 👇 Your turn.
Platform: #general · Best time: Afternoon
DAY 3 Value
Theme: Resource share
Found this resource and had to share it with you all. [Title/Link] [1-2 sentences on why it's useful] If you only have 10 minutes today, spend it on this. Let me know what you think after.
Platform: #resources · Best time: Morning
DAY 4 Activity
Theme: Low-friction poll
Quick poll for today: [Poll question with 2-4 options] [Optional: open-ended follow-up] Results in 24 hours. The winning option gets a deep dive post later this week.
Platform: #polls · Best time: Midday
DAY 5 Social
Theme: Member spotlight
👑 Member Spotlight: @[member name] [2-3 sentences about what they do, their recent contribution, something interesting about them] A few questions for you: 1. How did you get started in [field]? 2. What is your biggest challenge right now? 3. What's one thing the community can help you with? Drop your answers below!
Platform: #general · Schedule: 1/week, rotate members
DAY 6 Discussion
Theme: Hot take
Hot take (maybe unpopular): [Controversial opinion about the niche] Here is my reasoning: [1-2 sentences]. Do you agree or disagree? Tell me why. The best counter-argument gets a shoutout.
Platform: #discussions · Best time: Afternoon
DAY 7 Value
Theme: Weekly roundup
📬 Weekly Roundup - Week [X] This week in the community: - Best discussion: [link] - Most helpful member: @[name] - Top shared resource: [link] Next week's focus: [topic]. Keep showing up. See you Monday.
Platform: #announcements · Send: Friday evening
DAY 8 Welcome
Theme: New member batch
A big welcome to our newest members who joined this week! @[member1] @[member2] @[member3] Tell us: - Where are you from? - What's your biggest challenge right now? - What would make this community invaluable for you? Existing members: say hi and share one thing you love about this community.
Platform: #welcome · Best time: Monday morning
DAY 9 Activity
Theme: Challenge
🎯 7-Day Challenge: [Topic] Day 1 of 7: [small action] Your task today: [specific, takes <5 minutes]. Post your result in this thread. I will review and give feedback to everyone who completes it. Let's go!
Platform: #challenges · Duration: 7 days, auto-thread
DAY 10 Feedback
Theme: Community input
I want to make this community better for you. Honest answers only: What is one thing you wish we did more of? Less of? No wrong answers. I read every single response.
Platform: #feedback · Best time: Monthly
DAY 11 Discussion
Theme: Problem-solving
I am stuck on [problem] and would love your input. Here is the situation: [context]. What would you do? Has anyone here solved this before? Drop your advice below.
Platform: #help · Best time: Afternoon
DAY 12 Value
Theme: Template/checklist
I turned [process] into a simple checklist. Use it next time you [action]. [Link to template or checklist] Here is how it works: 1. Step one 2. Step two 3. Step three Save this. You will thank yourself later.
Platform: #resources · Best time: Morning
DAY 13 Social
Theme: Weekend fun
No work talk today. Just one question: What are you doing this weekend? Could be working on a project, spending time with family, or doing absolutely nothing. Share below.
Platform: #off-topic · Best time: Friday afternoon
DAY 14 Celebration
Theme: Win of the week
🏆 Win of the Week Thread Did you close a deal? Ship something? Learn something new? Finally fix that bug? Share your win below. Big or small, we celebrate it here. I will start: [your win].
Platform: #wins · Best time: Friday/Saturday
DAY 15 Discussion
Theme: Deep dive
Let's go deep on [topic] today. Here is what I know: [key insight or framework]. Here is what I am still figuring out: [open question]. What has your experience been? What works for you? What doesn't?
Platform: #deep-dives · Best time: Morning
DAY 16 Activity
Theme: Accountability
Accountability check-in: What is ONE thing you commit to doing this week related to [topic]? Post it here. Come back next week and tell us if you did it. I will check in with everyone who posts.
Platform: #accountability · Best time: Monday morning
DAY 17 Value
Theme: Book/article summary
I just finished [book/article] and here are my top 3 takeaways: 1. [Takeaway 1] 2. [Takeaway 2] 3. [Takeaway 3] If you only read one chapter, make it [chapter name]. Changed how I think about [topic].
Platform: #resources · Best time: Morning
DAY 18 Social
Theme: Meet your members
Let's do a quick connection exercise: Find someone in this thread who has a different role/industry than you. Reply to their intro and ask one question about their work. Goal: make at least one new connection today.
Platform: #introductions
DAY 19 Feedback
Theme: Feature request
If you could add ONE thing to this community, what would it be? - A weekly AMA? - A job board? - A resource library? - Office hours with me? Vote below or suggest something I haven't thought of.
Platform: #feedback
DAY 20 Discussion
Theme: Industry news
[Industry news headline] What does this mean for us? Here is my take: [2-3 sentences]. - Opportunity: [what this enables] - Risk: [what to watch out for] What is your take? Disagree? Let's discuss.
Platform: #discussions
DAY 21 Value
Theme: Case study
Case study: How [person/business] used [method] to get [result]. The setup: [situation]. What they did: [approach]. The result: [outcome]. Key lesson: [takeaway]. What would you have done differently?
Platform: #case-studies
DAY 22 Activity
Theme: Template swap
Template swap day! Share one template, checklist, or framework you use regularly. It can be anything: a project plan, a content calendar, a client questionnaire. I will start: [link to your template]. Browse what others shared and save what's useful.
Platform: #resources
DAY 23 Celebration
Theme: Milestone
🎉 We hit [number] members! When we started this community [X weeks/months ago], I hoped we would build something special. But this is beyond what I imagined. Thank you to every single person who has contributed, shared, asked questions, and helped others. This community exists because of you. To celebrate, [special offer/event/free resource].
Platform: #announcements
DAY 24 Discussion
Theme: AMA
Ask Me Anything! I am opening the floor for the next 2 hours. Ask me anything about [topic]. No question is too basic or too advanced. I will answer every single one.
Platform: #ama · Duration: 2 hours live
DAY 25 Value
Theme: Tool recommendation
Tool of the week: [Tool name] What it does: [1 sentence]. Who it is for: [audience]. Why I like it: [unique benefit]. Pricing: [free/paid/price]. Best alternative: [if applicable]. Have you used this? What's your experience?
Platform: #resources
DAY 26 Social
Theme: Show your setup
Show us your workspace! Desk setup, home office, coffee shop corner, or the chaos of your current project. Share a photo (or just describe it). Curious to see where everyone creates.
Platform: #off-topic
DAY 27 Activity
Theme: Co-working
🕒 Co-working session starting now! Post what you are working on in the next hour. I will be here working alongside you. Check in when you start. Check out when you finish. Let's get things done together.
Platform: #co-working · Best time: Morning (multiple time zones)
DAY 28 Feedback
Theme: Quarterly review
Quarterly community check-in: 1. What is the most valuable thing you have gained from this community? 2. What is one thing you wish existed but doesn't? 3. On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to recommend this community to a peer? Your answers shape what we do next quarter. Be honest.
Platform: #feedback · Frequency: Quarterly
DAY 29 Value
Theme: Guest expert
🎤 Special guest: @[expert name] [Expert] is joining us to share their expertise on [topic]. Drop your questions below. [Expert] will answer the top 5 most-upvoted questions in a thread later this week. Let's make this valuable for everyone.
Platform: #ama + #announcements
DAY 30 Celebration
Theme: Monthly recap
📊 Month in Review This month we: - Gained [X] new members - Had [X] discussions - Shared [X] resources - Helped [X] members with their questions Top contributor: @[name] Most popular post: [link] Best discussion: [link] Next month's focus: [topic]. Stay tuned. Thanks for being here. This community is nothing without you.
Platform: #announcements · Send: Last day of month
