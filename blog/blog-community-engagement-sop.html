Community Engagement SOP: How to Keep Members Active

TwoTopas · June 19, 2026 · 6 min read

Most online communities die within 90 days. Not because the topic isn't interesting — because nobody runs the daily operations.

This framework comes from Chinese private-domain operators who manage WeChat groups with thousands of members. They've turned community management into a repeatable system.

Tier 1: The Welcome Sequence (Days 1–3)

DayActionGoal
Day 1Welcome message + community rulesSet expectations
Day 2Personal intro promptFirst interaction
Day 3Share one valuable resourceDeliver value immediately

Tier 2: Daily Operations

TimeOpType
MorningDaily question / promptDiscussion
AfternoonShare one resource or insightValue
EveningHighlight best comment of the dayRecognition

Tier 3: Growth Loop (Month 2+)

  • Member highlight — showcase one member per week
  • AMAs / expert sessions — invite external experts
  • Member referral program — reward members who bring others
  • User-generated content — let members create the weekly digest
