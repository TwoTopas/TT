How to Build a Content Persona System

TwoTopas · June 19, 2026 · 5 min read

Most creators post randomly. One day a tutorial, next day a hot take, then silence for a week. Their audience never knows what to expect.

Chinese social media operators solve this with a Content Persona System — a framework that defines exactly what you post, when, and why.

The 4 Pillars

PillarPurposeExample
1. EducatorTeach something useful"How to price freelance projects"
2. StorytellerShare personal experience"How I lost a client and what I learned"
3. CuratorShare others' best content"3 tools every freelancer needs"
4. ProvocateurChallenge conventional wisdom"Hourly billing is killing your income"

Each piece of content fits into one pillar. Over a week, you rotate through all four. Your audience gets variety. You get a system.

The Weekly Rotation

DayPillarFormat
MondayEducatorTutorial / how-to
WednesdayStorytellerPersonal story
FridayCuratorResource roundup
SaturdayProvocateurOpinion / debate

The Content Audit

Before you start creating, audit your last 30 posts. Score each one against the 4 pillars. Most creators discover they only use 1 or 2 pillars — that's why their content feels repetitive.
